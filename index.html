About the Brand

A premium snack stop for every craving.

NachorrificbyCHUG offers delicious and affordable favorites like nachos, wings, crinkle mix, and more, all made from scratch using original recipes. It started as a simple home-based business in June 2019, built by a mother with a love for cooking and a dream of growing something of her own while caring for her child. From single orders to bulk orders, the business grew through passion, perseverance, and the support of satisfied customers. Today, NachorrificbyCHUG continues to bring comfort food cravings to life with bold flavors and a fun, modern touch.
